Psychosomatics table

Problem Psychosomatic Affirmation
Allergy (Hay Fever) Who can't you stand? Denial of one's own power. The world is not dangerous, it is a friend. I am not in any danger. I have no disagreement with life.
Angina You hold back on rude words. Feeling inability to express yourself I drop all restrictions and find freedom to be myself.
Insomnia Fear. Distrust of the life process. Guilt. With love I leave this day and give myself up to a peaceful sleep, knowing that tomorrow will take care of itself.
Bronchitis Nervous atmosphere in the family. Disputes and screams. A rare lull. I announce peace and harmony in and around me. Everything is going well.
Inflammation Fear. Fury. Sore consciousness. My thoughts are quiet, calm, concentrated.
Gastritis Lingering uncertainty. A sense of doom. I love and approve myself. I'm safe.
Headache Underestimating yourself. Self-criticism. Fear. I love and approve myself. I look at myself with love. I am completely safe.
Dizziness Fleeting, incoherent thoughts. Reluctance to see. In life, I am a calm and purposeful person. I can live perfectly and be happy.
Alona Rozlach
